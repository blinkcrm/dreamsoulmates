YOUR SAFETY MATTERS

Privacy Policy

Your data is protected. Your identity is safe. We take security seriously.

1. Introduction

DreamSoulmates ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile application, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK Data Protection Act 2018, and all applicable international privacy laws. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide:

  • Personal identification information (name, date of birth, age, gender)
  • Contact information (email address, phone number, mailing address)
  • Professional information (occupation, industry, income level)
  • Identity verification documents (passport scans, residency permits)
  • Profile information (community, religion, caste, education)
  • Kundali information (birth date, time, location)
  • Preferences for potential matches
  • Profile photos and videos
  • Communication history with other users

2.2 Information Automatically Collected:

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, features used)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies
  • Location data (if permitted)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  • Providing and improving our matching and AI recommendation services
  • Verifying user identity and preventing fraud
  • Processing payments and sending billing information
  • Sending promotional emails, newsletters, and service updates
  • Responding to inquiries and customer service requests
  • Kundali analysis and success prediction calculations
  • Conducting research and analytics to improve our Service
  • Complying with legal obligations and law enforcement requests
  • Protecting our rights and preventing misuse of the Service

4. Identity Verification & Document Security

As part of our commitment to 100% verified profiles:

  • We collect scans or images of government-issued documents (passport, residency permits)
  • Our verification team manually reviews all documents within 48-72 hours
  • If verification fails, documents are securely deleted and not retained
  • Verified users are marked with a verification badge
  • Document images are encrypted and stored securely
  • Only authorized personnel can access verification documents

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We DO share your information with:

  • Other users (based on your privacy preferences and profile visibility settings)
  • Service providers and vendors (payment processors, email providers, hosting services)
  • Legal authorities when required by law or court order

We DO NOT share your information with:

  • Third-party advertisers or marketing companies (unless you opt-in)
  • Data brokers or information resellers
  • Companies outside the approved vendor ecosystem

6. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure password hashing with industry-standard algorithms
  • Multi-factor authentication available for accounts
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Restricted access to personal data (need-to-know basis)
  • Secure backup and disaster recovery procedures
  • Employee confidentiality and data protection training

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are a resident of the EU, UK, or other jurisdictions with similar data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Portability: Receive data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@dreamsoulmates.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Photo & Profile Privacy

You have full control over your profile visibility:

  • Keep photos private and show only to specific matches you approve
  • Hide your profile from search results
  • Restrict who can contact you
  • Set preferences for which information is visible to other users
  • Remove or delete your profile at any time

9. Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login preferences
  • Understand how you use our Service
  • Improve your user experience
  • Analyze traffic patterns

You can control cookie settings through your browser. However, disabling cookies may affect Service functionality.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for:

  • Active accounts: Duration of your account + 90 days after deletion
  • Verification documents: While account is active; deleted upon account closure
  • Payment records: 7 years (for tax and legal compliance)
  • Chat history: Until account deletion (users can delete conversations)
  • Marketing data: Until you unsubscribe

11. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

12. Children's Privacy

DreamSoulmates is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we become aware of data collection from minors, we will delete such information immediately.

13. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred, processed, and stored in countries other than your country of residence, including countries that may not have the same data protection standards. By using our Service, you consent to such transfers. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place.

14. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and regulatory authorities within 72 hours as required by law. We will provide details about the breach, affected data, and remediation measures.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or by posting the updated policy on our website with a new effective date. Your continued use of the Service indicates acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

16. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

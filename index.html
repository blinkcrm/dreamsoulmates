GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is UKLife.in free to use?
Yes! Creating a profile, getting verified, and browsing AI matches is completely free. To contact matches, attend events, or access advanced features like detailed Kundali reports, you can upgrade to Premium (£29/month) or VIP (£99/month). Cancel anytime.
How does verification work?
We verify identity using Aadhaar, PAN Card, Passport, and UK Visa documents. Our team manually checks every submission. Only verified profiles appear in search results. This ensures zero fake profiles on the platform.
Can parents register on behalf of their children?
Absolutely! Many profiles on UKLife.in are created by parents, siblings, or relatives. We have a dedicated "Profile For" option during registration — choose Son, Daughter, Brother, Sister, or Relative. Parents in India can also register for their children living in UK.
Can I match with someone in India (not just UK)?
Yes! While our primary focus is Indians in the UK, many NRIs prefer to match with someone in India who is willing to relocate. You can set your preference to "UK Only", "India Only", or "Both" in your profile settings.
How do I report a fake or suspicious profile?
Every profile has a "Report" button. You can also email our safety team at safety@uklife.in. We investigate every report within 24 hours and take strict action — suspicious profiles are permanently removed.
Is my data safe? Are you GDPR compliant?
Yes, we are fully GDPR and UK Data Protection Act 2018 compliant. Your data is encrypted with 256-bit SSL, stored securely, and never shared with third parties. You can request data deletion at any time from your account settings.

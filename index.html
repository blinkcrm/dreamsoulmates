GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

How does verification work?
We verify identity using Passport and Residents Permits documents. Our team manually checks every submission. Only verified profiles appear in search results. This ensures zero fake profiles on the platform.
Can parents register on behalf of their children?
Absolutely! Many profiles on DreamSoulmates are created by parents, siblings, or relatives. We have a dedicated "Profile For" option during registration — choose Son, Daughter, Brother, Sister, or Relative. Parents can register for their children globally.
Can I match with NRIs across the globe?
Absolutely! DreamSoulmates connects NRIs across America, EMEA, APAC and globally. You can set your preference for countries and regions in your profile settings to match with compatible NRIs anywhere in the world.
Is my data safe? Are you GDPR compliant?
Yes, we are fully GDPR and UK Data Protection Act 2018 compliant. Your data is encrypted with 256-bit SSL, stored securely, and never shared with third parties. You can request data deletion at any time from your account settings.
